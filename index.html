Guild Description

Our guild is a competitive guild with players that spend most of our time playing bedwars. We are a social and friendly guild called The Waves! Our guild has been arround since Bed Wars came out and we have grown over time. We strive to become a guild where we respect one another and treat each other fairly. We don't discriminate between high stars and low stars, all players are equal in this guild.

Hypixel Competetive League

We are now a part of the Hypixel Competetive League [HCL]. It is an association of guilds that are coming together to compete through multiple PvP minigames and see who can emerge victorious. You may think, "isn't this an alliance?" No, think of HCL as your favorite E-Sport. We are teams coming together in hopes of both showing off our skill and to unite the community. We’re here to have a huge impact on the server, and to bring back the era of macro guilds. This entire project will be community led, and all guilds apart of this league will have a say in how we run various things! For now, we’ll be focusing on PvP oriented games, but we’ll be swiftly moving towards all games across the Network!
