General

The Waves is a competitive guild with players that spend most of their time playing BedWars or UHC. We are a social and friendly guild and have been around since Bed Wars came out and we have grown over time. We strive to become a guild where we respect one another and treat each other fairly. We don't discriminate between high stars and low stars, all players are equal in this guild.
